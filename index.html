BIOFEEDBACK: A technology studied, approved, and safe

Biofeedback is a therapy solution brought back in 1969, aiming to combine therapy and the new word of cybernetics. The process involves treatment designed to allow self-regulation for the subject of various bodily functions through electronic monitoring of bodily functions, and to share this monitoring with the subject.

An open communication with the subject is provided.
The Cost:
No negative side affects have been reported
